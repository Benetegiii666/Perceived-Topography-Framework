# Research Goals

## Primary Goal
Develop and articulate a coherent theoretical framework for how perceived topography shapes behavior across organizational, AI, and human decision-making contexts.

## Secondary Goals
- Establish clear definitions and a conceptual hierarchy
- Map relationships between perception, topography, governance, and emergent behavior
- Identify and preserve fault lines where the theory is under tension
- Explore the connection between agentic retrieval, state, and perception construction
- Produce a publishable paper that communicates the theory effectively

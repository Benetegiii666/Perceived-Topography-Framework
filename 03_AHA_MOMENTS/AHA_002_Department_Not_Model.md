# AHA_002: The Department, Not the Model

**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)

## The Moment

> "The danger isn't the model. It's the department."

The realization that organizational structure around AI agents poses a greater risk than the agent's capabilities. The department sets objectives, controls information flow, and defines the topography the agent navigates.

## Why It Matters

Reframes AI risk from a technical problem (model safety) to an organizational design problem (departmental topography). This connects AI governance to decades of organizational behavior research.

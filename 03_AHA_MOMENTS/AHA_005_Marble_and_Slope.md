# AHA_005: Stop Looking at the Marble. Look at the Slope.

**Paper Status:** Explicitly promoted — marble metaphor appears in Section 1
**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)

## The Moment

> "Stop looking at the marble. Look at the slope."

The realization that studying the agent (marble) in isolation misses the point. The behavior is determined by the landscape (slope). To understand behavior, study the environment.

## Why It Matters

This is the framework's core metaphor in its most compact form. It redirects attention from agent properties to environmental properties. In AI terms: stop studying the model's weights and start studying the context it navigates. In organizational terms: stop profiling employees and start examining the structures they work within.

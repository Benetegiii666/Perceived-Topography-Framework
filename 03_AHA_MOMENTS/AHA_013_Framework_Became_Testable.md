# AHA_013: The Framework Became Testable

**Date:** 2026-06-09

## The Moment

The Hidden Risk / Misread Signal / Attention Failure model gave us something we didn't previously have: **a way to be wrong.**

Specifically: if layer-targeted interventions do not outperform unrelated interventions, the layers may not be real.

That transformed CA_005 from a defensive critique into a **research instrument**.

## Why It Matters

This is the moment the project shifted from:

> "This is an interesting way of looking at the world."

to:

> "This might eventually be a useful way of investigating the world."

Those are very different things. The first is a perspective. The second is a research program.

The co-author identified this as the most important moment of the session — not because of what it says about the framework, but because of what it says about the framework's maturity. A theory that can articulate its own failure conditions has crossed a threshold that most conceptual frameworks never reach.

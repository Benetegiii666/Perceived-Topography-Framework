# AHA_011: The Marketing VP Is the Human Analog of the Agent Problem

**Date:** 2026-06-09

## The Moment

The same structure explains both:

**Marketing VP:** Goal → Visibility → Decision

**AI Agent:** Goal → Visibility → Behavior

The agent discussion stopped feeling exotic. The marketing example made it obvious.

## Why It Matters

This became one of the strongest explanatory bridges in the repository. It demonstrates that the framework is not an AI-specific theory with organizational analogies — it is a general systems model where AI and human decision-making are instances of the same pattern.

When a VP builds a campaign brief from a knowledge platform, they are doing exactly what an AI agent does when constructing a response from a context window: optimizing within a perceived landscape shaped by the information surfaces available to them.

# AHA_004: What Reality Are We Allowing the Agent to Perceive?

**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)

## The Moment

> "What reality are we allowing the agent to perceive?"

The shift from "what should we tell the agent to do?" to "what should the agent perceive?" This reframes the design question from instruction to perception design.

## Why It Matters

This is the bridge between topography and perception. It implies that:
1. Perception is not given — it is *allowed* or *designed*
2. The designer of the information surface has power over behavior
3. The agent's reality is a subset of actual reality, filtered through whatever surfaces are available

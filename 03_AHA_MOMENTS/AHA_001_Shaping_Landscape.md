# AHA_001: Shaping the Landscape

**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)
**Role:** THE ORIGIN SENTENCE
**Paper Status:** Explicitly promoted — appears in Section 1 — first major phase transition in the discussion

## The Moment

> "As agent ecosystems become more sophisticated, the challenge may become less about rewarding agents and more about shaping the landscape they optimize within."

This is the sentence that shifted the conversation from alignment-as-reward-design to alignment-as-environment-design. Everything that followed — topography, perception, information surfaces — traces back to this reframe.

## Why It Matters

Before this moment, the discussion was about how to constrain or reward agents. After it, the discussion became about how to design the environments agents navigate. That's not a refinement — it's a category shift.

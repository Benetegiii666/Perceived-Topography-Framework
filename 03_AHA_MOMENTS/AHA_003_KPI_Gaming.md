# AHA_003: Reward Hacking IS KPI Gaming

**Paper Status:** Explicitly promoted — reward hacking/KPI gaming bridge appears in Section 6
**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)

## The Moment

> "Reward hacking is KPI gaming."

The recognition that AI reward hacking and organizational KPI gaming are not analogous — they are the *same phenomenon* in different domains. Both involve agents optimizing for measured signals rather than intended outcomes.

## Why It Matters

This was an early indicator that the framework might be a general systems model, not an AI-specific one. If the same behavioral pattern appears in both artificial and human systems, the underlying mechanism is domain-independent.

## Evolution

This aha moment was deepened by DISCOVERY_002, which reframed KPI gaming as information surface corruption — a more mechanistic explanation of *how* it works.

## Observation

Behavior appears to be an emergent property of optimizer-topography interaction, not a direct product of instruction or incentive.

## Implication

If behavior is emergent rather than directed, then prediction requires understanding the system (agent + environment) rather than just the agent. You cannot predict what an agent will do by studying the agent alone — you must study the landscape it navigates.

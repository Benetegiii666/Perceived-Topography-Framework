# DISCOVERY_006: AI as Example of Broader Phenomenon

**Status:** Open
**Date:** 2026-06-09 (from seed material)

## Observation

AI may be the newest example of a broader systems phenomenon, not a unique case. The patterns we observe in AI agent behavior — optimization within perceived environments, reward hacking, sensitivity to context — appear to be instances of the same dynamics seen in organizations, markets, and individual decision-making.

## Implication

If AI is an example rather than the phenomenon, then:
1. AI safety insights may apply to organizational design and vice versa
2. The framework is a general systems model, not an AI-specific one
3. AI systems may be the clearest *demonstration* of the framework because their information surfaces are explicitly engineered (unlike human systems where they're emergent)

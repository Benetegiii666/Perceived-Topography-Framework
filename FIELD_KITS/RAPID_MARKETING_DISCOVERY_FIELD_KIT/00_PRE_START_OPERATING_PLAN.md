## Pre-Start Goals

Before July 13, 2026, Benet should aim to:

1. Meet with Jeremy, Head of Marketing Strategy.
2. Record the conversation. The transcript will be used to fill discovery forms.
3. Sanity-check whether campaign brief creation is the right first workflow.
4. Identify a recent or upcoming campaign that could serve as the first discovery case.
5. Learn what marketing strategy cares about most.
6. Identify existing artifacts: briefs, ICPs, messaging, brand rules, claims, performance reports, sales feedback.
7. Learn who should be involved after the official start date.
8. Identify cultural or political sensitivities.
9. Avoid over-presenting the architecture before understanding the workflow.

## What To Avoid Before July 13

Do not:

- Ask Martin or Cassidy for infrastructure review yet
- Ask marketing stakeholders to validate the HLD
- Present the full Perceived Topography Framework unless asked
- Create schemas from assumptions
- Create implementation backlog from assumptions
- Make stack decisions final
- Imply that the prototype is already designed
- Position the effort as process evaluation or judgment

## Recommended Pre-Start Sequence

1. Review the field kit.
2. Meet with Jeremy (record the meeting).
3. Provide the transcript to Claude for processing into discovery forms.
4. Capture post-meeting notes using `08_POST_JEREMY_NOTES_TEMPLATE.md`.
5. Identify whether to use a recent campaign or upcoming campaign for formal discovery.
6. Prepare for 1-2 stakeholder interviews after official start.
7. After July 13, run formal discovery using the questionnaire.
8. Fill the Campaign Brief Workflow Discovery worksheet from transcripts and notes.
9. Then proceed toward data model sketch, delivery architecture refinement, backlog, and prototype.

## Transcript Processing Protocol

Benet will record the Jeremy meeting and provide the transcript. The transcript should be used to:

- Fill the post-Jeremy notes template (`08_POST_JEREMY_NOTES_TEMPLATE.md`)
- Extract answers for Campaign Brief Workflow Discovery Categories 1-3 where Jeremy provides relevant information
- Identify information surfaces mentioned
- Identify stakeholders for later conversations
- Identify campaign candidates for formal discovery

The transcript should NOT be used to:

- Finalize discovery answers without Benet's review
- Create schemas or data models
- Make architecture decisions
- Fill in answers Jeremy did not provide (mark those as still TBD)

## Working Principle

The pre-start goal is to reduce ambiguity, not to finalize the solution.

The strongest outcome before July 13 is:

- A good first campaign candidate
- A clear sense of marketing strategy's needs
- A list of artifacts to request later
- A stakeholder map
- A better understanding of where AI support would be useful rather than burdensome
